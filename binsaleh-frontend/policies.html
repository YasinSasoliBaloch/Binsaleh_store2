Overview

BIN SALEH Store ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you shop with us or contact us through our website or social media channels.

By placing an order or using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you place an order, contact our support team, or interact with us on social media:

  • Full name and contact number
  • Delivery address (city, area, street)
  • Order details and product preferences
  • Messages sent via WhatsApp, Instagram, or our website
  • Payment confirmation details (advance transfer receipts)
🔒 We do not collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details directly.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to process and deliver your order, and to provide customer support:

  • To confirm and process your order
  • To coordinate delivery with our courier partners
  • To contact you regarding order updates or exchanges
  • To resolve any disputes or complaints
  • To improve our products and customer experience
🚫 We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

We share your delivery details (name, address, phone number) only with our trusted courier partners responsible for delivering your order. These partners are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure. Order information is handled by our team with strict confidentiality.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. Please contact us immediately if you suspect any misuse of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data after your order is fulfilled
  • Withdraw your consent to be contacted for non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on WhatsApp at +9710566551046 or email binsalehllc946@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Overview

At BIN SALEH Store, customer satisfaction is our priority. We want you to love what you receive. This policy outlines when and how you may exchange a product, and the conditions under which refunds may be considered.

📦 Please read this policy carefully before placing your order. By completing a purchase, you agree to these terms.

Exchange Policy

We offer a straightforward exchange process. Exchanges are accepted within 7 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • The item is unused, unwashed, and in its original condition
  • Tags and packaging are intact
  • The item was not purchased during a sale or promotional event
  • You have your order confirmation or proof of purchase

To initiate an exchange, contact us on WhatsApp within the 7-day window with your order details and photos of the item.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please:

  • Take clear photos of the item and packaging immediately upon receiving
  • Contact us on WhatsApp at +9710566551046 within 48 hours of delivery
  • Share your order number and photos with our team
✅ Upon verification, we will dispatch a replacement item at no additional cost to you.

Refund Policy

We currently operate on an exchange-first basis. Cash refunds are only issued in the following circumstances:

  • The item you ordered is out of stock and no suitable exchange is available
  • A confirmed duplicate payment was made by the customer
  • We are unable to fulfill your order after receiving the advance payment
💳 Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 working days via the same payment method — bank account, EasyPaisa, or JazzCash wallet.

Refunds are not issued for: change of mind, incorrect size selected by the customer, or items showing signs of use or washing.

Non-Exchangeable Items

The following items are not eligible for exchange or refund under any circumstances:

  • Fragrances and perfumes (once opened)
  • Innerwear and socks (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items purchased during clearance or final-sale promotions
  • Customised or personalised products
  • Gift cards or vouchers

Advance Payment & Cancellations

A confirmation advance of Rs. 250 is required to secure your order. This amount is deducted from your total bill upon delivery.

If you wish to cancel your order before it has been dispatched, contact us immediately — the full advance will be refunded.

⚠️ Cancellations after dispatch are not eligible for advance refund, as courier and handling costs have already been incurred. Repeated cancellations may result in future order restrictions.

How to Initiate a Request

To begin an exchange or refund request:

  • Contact us on WhatsApp: +9710566551046
  • Provide your order number and a brief description of the issue
  • Share clear photos of the item(s) in question
  • Our team will respond within 24 hours to guide you through the next steps
